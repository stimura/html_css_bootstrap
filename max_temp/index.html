Latitude vs. Max Temperature:

My analysis into the relationship between a particular city's latitude and the maximum temperature the city reached during the year, rendered a relationship that is not surprising. The temperature of the city is negatively correlated with the city’s latitude. One can see the negative relationship quite clearly in the chart on the left with the line of best fit. This makes complete sense, the closer the city is to the poles – North Pole or South Pole – the colder the temperature will be on average. Likewise, if the city is closer to the equator, the city would experience higher temperatures on average.
