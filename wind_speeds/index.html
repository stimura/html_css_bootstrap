Latitude vs. Wind Speeds:

I did not have particular starting hypothesis for the relationship between latitude and wind speed, but nonetheless, the results of my analysis was surprising. There is a evident relationship between latitude and wind speed depicted on the right chart. As one can see, the line of best fit is upward sloping. This upward sloping line indicates that there is a positive relationship, such that the greater the latitude, the higher wind speeds that particular area will experience. I am currently researching why this relationship exists, and will update this page as soon as I receive a definitive answer.
